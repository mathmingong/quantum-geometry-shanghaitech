Speaker

Quantum geometry: from invariants to categories, and back.

📅 July 5–10, 2026📍 ShanghaiTech University

📐 About

The conference aims to bring together scholars working on enumerative invariants, categorical invariants, mirror symmetry, algebraic geometry and symplectic geometry, to showcase the latest research progress in these areas, share recent research findings, stimulate innovative ideas, and conduct in-depth discussions on the frontier research directions in these fields.


Organizers: Qile Chen (Boston College), Bohan Fang (Peking University), Yunfeng Jiang (University of Kansas), Junwu Tu (ShanghaiTech).
